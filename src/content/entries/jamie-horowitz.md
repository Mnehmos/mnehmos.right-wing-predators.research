## Description

Jamie Horowitz was Fox Sports President who was forced to resign in 2017 after sexual harassment allegations. He was known for hiring hosts who used stereotypes to appeal to Trump voters. Multiple complaints about his behavior led to his termination. Horowitz had created a workplace culture that prioritized provocative content over professional conduct.
