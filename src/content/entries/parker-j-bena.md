## Overview

Republican activist Parker J. Bena was a presidential elector for George W. Bush in 2000. In 2001, he pleaded guilty to possession of child pornography, including images of children as young as 3 years old, on his home computer. He was sentenced to 30 months in federal prison and fined $18,000.
