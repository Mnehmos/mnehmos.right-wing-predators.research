## Description

Dinesh D'Souza is a conservative author and filmmaker convicted of campaign finance violations. His ex-wife Dixie D'Souza wrote a letter to the court alleging domestic violence during their marriage. She described incidents of physical and emotional abuse. D'Souza has been a prominent conservative voice and received a presidential pardon from Trump in 2018.
