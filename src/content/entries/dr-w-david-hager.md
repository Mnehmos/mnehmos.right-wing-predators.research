## Description

Dr. W. David Hager is a prominent obstetrician-gynecologist from Kentucky and anti-abortion advocate appointed by the Bush administration to the Reproductive Health Drugs Advisory Committee of the FDA in 2002. According to his ex-wife Linda Carruth Davis, Hager repeatedly sodomized her while she slept between 1995 and their divorce in 2002.
