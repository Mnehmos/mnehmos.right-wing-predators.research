## Description

Chad Lupinacci was Huntington Supervisor and former Republican New York State Assemblyman who faced sexual assault allegations from a former male staffer. Lupinacci is famously anti-gay, making the allegations particularly ironic. The accuser filed a lawsuit alleging unwanted sexual contact. A trial was scheduled to proceed on the assault charges.
