## Description

Anthony Moore worked at The Village Church, an anti-gay conservative evangelical church, where he was fired for secretly filming his youth pastor in the shower. Despite his disturbing behavior, Cedarville University gave him a second chance and hired him as a professor. When the university learned the full extent of his misconduct and that they had been lied to, they fired Moore and the president apologized.
