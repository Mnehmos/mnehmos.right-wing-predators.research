## Description

James Manning is an infamous homophobic reverend and conspiracy theorist who has been an intermittent Trump supporter. He runs ATLAH World Missionary Church in Harlem and was accused of abusing students at the private school within his church. Former students came forward with allegations of physical and emotional abuse. Manning has promoted numerous conspiracy theories and extreme anti-LGBTQ rhetoric.
