## Description

Don Haidl was an assistant sheriff in Orange County, California. His son, Gregory Haidl (age 19 at the time), was one of three men charged in the July 2002 sexual assault of a 16-year-old girl. The victim was drugged and assaulted while unconscious on a pool table; she was penetrated with a lighted cigarette and pool cue, and the assault was videotaped. The first trial ended in a mistrial in June 2004 due to jury deadlock. In the retrial, Gregory Haidl and two co-defendants were convicted in March 2005. Gregory received a six-year prison sentence in 2006. Don Haidl used his position as sheriff to smear the victim and defend his son's actions, claiming the victim "deserved it" because she was a "slut."
