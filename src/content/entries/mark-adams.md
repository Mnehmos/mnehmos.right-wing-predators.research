## Description

Mark Adams was Butler Township fiscal officer and Republican county treasurer in Ohio who resigned after sexual harassment allegations. Multiple complaints were filed against Adams for inappropriate conduct. The township accepted his resignation following an investigation. Adams had been a Republican party official in the area.
