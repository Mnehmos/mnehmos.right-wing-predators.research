## Description

Greg May was the businessman featured in Republican Kentucky Governor Matt Bevin's campaign advertisement. It was revealed that May had settled two sexual misconduct lawsuits. The Republican Governors Association pulled the advertisement after the settlements became public. The incident embarrassed the Bevin campaign during his reelection bid.
