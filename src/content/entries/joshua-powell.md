## Description

Joshua Powell was former NRA executive director of general operations and a close advisor to Wayne LaPierre. He quietly settled at least two sexual harassment complaints during his tenure at the organization. The settlements were kept confidential until investigative reporting exposed them in 2019. Powell's misconduct was part of broader allegations of corruption and misconduct at NRA leadership.
