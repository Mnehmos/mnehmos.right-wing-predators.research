## Description

Conrad Estrada Valdez, 61, pastor at The Restoration Outreach Christian Church in Houston, Texas, pleaded guilty in 2021 to sexual assault of a child between ages 14 and 17. In 2019, a then-30-year-old woman disclosed that Valdez had sexually abused her when she was 15. She described him as a longtime family friend and pastor/mentor. She had initially sought counseling from him after experiencing previous sexual assault. Valdez was sentenced to 14 years in prison.
