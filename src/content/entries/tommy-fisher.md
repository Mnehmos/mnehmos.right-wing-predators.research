## Description

Tommy Fisher runs Fisher Industries, which became Trump's favorite contractor for building the border wall. Fisher was accused of sexual harassment of employees. He took over the company after his brother David Fisher, the former CEO, was sentenced to prison for child pornography. The company received major contracts despite the family's history.
