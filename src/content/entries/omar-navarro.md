## Description

Omar Navarro was a Republican congressional candidate from California who ran against Maxine Waters. He was accused of hiring far-right operative Jacob Wohl to harass and threaten his ex-girlfriend and a former staffer. The allegations included stalking and intimidation tactics. Navarro had multiple restraining orders filed against him by women.
