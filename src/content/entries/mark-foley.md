## Overview

U.S. Representative Mark Foley (R-FL) resigned from Congress on September 29, 2006, after it was revealed he had sent sexually explicit instant messages and emails to teenage boys who had served as congressional pages.

## Description

U.S. Representative Mark Foley (R-FL) resigned from Congress on September 29, 2006, after it was revealed he had sent sexually explicit instant messages and emails to teenage boys who had served as congressional pages. The scandal broke less than six weeks before the 2006 midterm elections.
