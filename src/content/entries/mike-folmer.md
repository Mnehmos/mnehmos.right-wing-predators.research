## Description

Mike Folmer was a Pennsylvania Republican state senator who resigned in 2019 after being charged with possession of child pornography. He was arrested after police found images on his phone during an investigation. Folmer had represented the 48th district and sat on the state government committee. He pleaded guilty in 2020 and was sentenced to prison.
