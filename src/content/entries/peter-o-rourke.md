## Description

Peter O'Rourke was Florida GOP Executive Director who previously worked at the Department of Veterans Affairs. He was accused of retaliating against whistleblowers, covering up sexual harassment complaints, and political meddling at the VA. Multiple investigations documented his misconduct. O'Rourke's actions created a toxic work environment at the VA.
