## Description

Stacey Dash is a D-list actress who became a vocal Trump supporter and Fox News contributor. She was arrested for domestic violence in 2019 after allegedly pushing and slapping her husband during an argument. Dash claimed self-defense. She had previously run as a Republican candidate for Congress in California.
