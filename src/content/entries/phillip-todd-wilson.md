## Description

Phillip Todd Wilson was an anti-gay Kentucky high school principal who banned books with LGBTQ content, claiming they were pornographic. In 2019, he was charged with 30 counts of possessing and sharing child pornography. The irony was not lost on critics who noted he had censored books with gay characters while possessing actual child exploitation materials. Wilson's case exposed stunning hypocrisy.
