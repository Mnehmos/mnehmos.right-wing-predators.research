## Description

Corey Lewandowski served as Donald Trump's 2016 campaign manager. He was charged with battery in 2016 after assaulting a reporter, though charges were later dropped. In 2017, he was accused of sexual assault by a singer. In 2021, multiple women accused him of making unwanted sexual advances, including groping a donor's wife at a Las Vegas charity event. Despite multiple accusations, he remained a Trump advisor.
