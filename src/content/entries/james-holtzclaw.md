## Description

James Holtzclaw was an Idaho Republican state representative accused of sexual harassment by multiple women. The allegations involved inappropriate comments and unwanted advances. An investigation found credible evidence of misconduct. Holtzclaw faced calls to resign but initially resisted stepping down from his position.
