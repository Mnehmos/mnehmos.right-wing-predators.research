## Overview

Jeffrey Epstein is the central subject of the released archive, which documents allegations, court filings, travel records, and investigative material tied to his sexual abuse and trafficking of minors.

## Description

The archive's Palm Beach probable-cause affidavits describe a pattern in which minors were recruited to Jeffrey Epstein's residence for paid "massages" that escalated into sexual abuse. Additional court complaints in the archive allege sexual assault and abuse by Epstein, while federal docket material, private-aircraft records, and later travel-history files document the legal and logistical footprint of his operation. This entry is based on the released files themselves and treats Epstein as the primary perpetrator around whom the broader archive network is organized.
