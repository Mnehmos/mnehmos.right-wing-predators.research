## Description

Eric Firkel was former Cattaraugus County attorney in New York and a failed candidate for the Republican primary for county legislature. He was accused of sexual harassment that led to an ethics investigation. The allegations involved inappropriate conduct with female employees. Firkel resigned amid the investigation.
