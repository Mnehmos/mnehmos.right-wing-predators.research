## Description

U.S. Senator Strom Thurmond had sex with Carrie Butler, a 15-year-old Black housemaid working for his family, when he was 22 years old in 1925. Their daughter, Essie Mae Washington-Williams, was born in October 1925. Thurmond, who became known for pro-segregation politics, kept his daughter's existence secret until after his death in 2003.
