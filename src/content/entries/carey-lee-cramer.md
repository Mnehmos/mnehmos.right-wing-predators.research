## Overview

GOP advertising consultant Carey Lee Cramer created anti-Gore ads and used his 9-year-old stepdaughter in a 2000 Republican commercial. He was arrested in 2005 and convicted in 2006 of aggravated sexual assault of a child, two counts of indecency with a child by contact, and indecency with a child by exposure.

## Description

GOP advertising consultant Carey Lee Cramer created anti-Gore ads and used his 9-year-old stepdaughter in a 2000 Republican commercial. He was arrested in 2005 and convicted in 2006 of aggravated sexual assault of a child, two counts of indecency with a child by contact, and indecency with a child by exposure. The case was in McAllen/Mercedes, Texas.
