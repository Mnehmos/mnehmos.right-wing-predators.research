## Description

Brown County Wisconsin Republican Party Chairman Donald Fleischman was charged in September 2007 with child enticement, contributing to delinquency of a child, and exposing his genitals. Police found a 16-year-old boy hiding in his closet in November 2006. Charges were dropped then refiled in February 2008. He stepped down as county GOP chair and the party disbanded after it couldn't find new leadership.
