## Description

Republican election board official Kevin Coan was arrested in 2004 for soliciting sex over the internet from what he believed was a 13-year-old girl. The sting operation was conducted by law enforcement, and Coan was caught attempting to meet the supposed minor for sexual purposes. He pleaded guilty and was sentenced to two years probation. The case highlighted the vulnerability of online systems to predatory behavior by officials in positions of public trust.
