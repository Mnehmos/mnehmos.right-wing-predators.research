## Description

Robert Foster was a Mississippi Republican gubernatorial candidate who refused to allow a female journalist to cover his campaign unless she brought a male chaperone. He claimed he needed protection from false accusations, promoting rape culture stereotypes. The incident sparked national debate about misogyny and Mike Pence-style policies. Foster's campaign defended the decision as protecting his marriage and reputation.
