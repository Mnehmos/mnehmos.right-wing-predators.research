## Overview

Sarah Kellen appears in the released archive as a Jeffrey Epstein assistant and alleged co-defendant in the recruitment and abuse of minors.

## Description

Palm Beach probable-cause affidavits in the archive describe Sarah Kellen as an Epstein assistant involved in the recruitment pipeline that brought minors to Epstein's residence for unlawful sexual activity. The same archive material says charges were considered against Kellen as a principal in the first degree, while police reports list her among the suspects in the sexual-battery investigation. Additional telephone records and court filings in the released files place Kellen inside the operational side of Epstein's abuse network rather than outside it.
