## Description

Michael Ferro was Chicago Tribune owner and Republican donor (30 of 34 donations went to Republicans) who faced sexual misconduct allegations. Multiple women accused him of inappropriate behavior. Ferro resigned as chairman of Tronc in 2018 amid the allegations. He had been a significant financial supporter of Republican causes.
