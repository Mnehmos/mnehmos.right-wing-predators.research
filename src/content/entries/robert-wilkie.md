## Description

Robert Wilkie was Trump's Secretary of Veterans Affairs who refused to recognize sexual violence at VA facilities. When a female veteran reported being sexually assaulted at a VA hospital, Wilkie accused her of lying despite one in four women veterans experiencing sexual violence or harassment at VA facilities. He attempted to discredit the victim rather than address systemic problems.
