## Description

Bernie Alimenti, Republican Councilman in Fort Meyers, pleaded no contest to offering influence in exchange for sex. This is after it was also revealed he had been fired from the lottery for sexual harassment. When the Clemency Board refused to restore his voting rights, he threatened to leave the GOP and become independent.
