## Description

Ronny Jackson was White House physician under Trump who later became a Republican congressman from Texas. Multiple allegations surfaced of sexual harassment, creating a hostile work environment, and inappropriate conduct while serving as White House doctor. A scathing Pentagon Inspector General report in 2021 found he engaged in inappropriate conduct including sexual harassment and drinking on duty. Despite the allegations, he was elected to Congress.
