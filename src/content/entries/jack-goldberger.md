## Overview

Jack Goldberger appears in the released archive as one of Jeffrey Epstein's local defense attorneys during the plea fight, victim-deposition disputes, and witness-pressure phase of the case.

## Description

Archive records repeatedly identify Jack Goldberger as Epstein's lawyer during the period when Epstein's team was negotiating a lenient resolution and pressing to manage the fallout from the case. A Palm Beach police incident report in the archive describes alleged harassment connected to a private investigator hired by Epstein while Goldberger was representing him, and other released documents place Goldberger in deposition fights, scheduling disputes, and the broader defense effort that delayed and narrowed accountability. This entry treats Goldberger as an archive-backed shield figure in the legal apparatus around Epstein.
