## Description

Monica Wehby was a Republican Oregon Senate candidate who faced allegations of stalking and harassment from an ex-boyfriend and ex-husband. Police reports documented incidents of unwanted contact and disturbing behavior. The allegations emerged during her 2014 Senate campaign. Wehby denied wrongdoing but the revelations damaged her candidacy.
