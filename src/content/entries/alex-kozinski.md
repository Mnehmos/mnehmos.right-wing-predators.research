## Description

Republican Judge Alex Kozinski, Brett Kavanaugh's mentor, faced multiple allegations of sexual harassment. Kozinski resigned from the bench in 2008 after a judicial investigation revealed he had stored sexually explicit images on his personal website and shown them to colleagues and law clerks.
