## Description

R. A. Strickland was Colleton County Sheriff in South Carolina who was arrested in 2019 for punching a woman in the face during a domestic violence incident. Despite being a law enforcement officer sworn to protect citizens, he was charged with criminal domestic violence. The arrest shocked the community and raised questions about his fitness for office.
