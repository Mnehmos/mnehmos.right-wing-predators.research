## Description

Brad Namdar was a GOP Congressional primary candidate in TX-32. He was also an outspoken advocate against human trafficking. He lost his endorsements & was asked to leave anti-trafficking organization after an escort told media he hired her twice & didn't pay for the second. He is also alleged to have inappropriately touched a student who was under age.
