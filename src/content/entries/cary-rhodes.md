## Description

Cary Rhodes, Republican mayor of Taylorsville, GA was convicted of trying to have sex with a child in a internet sting. He received two years in prison and 10 years on probation on one count of computer and electronic child exploitation. He must also pay a $2,000 fine plus court costs and surcharges, perform 240 hours of community service and abide by sex offender restrictions avoiding all children except grandchildren, nieces, and nephews (with another adult present).
