## Description

Jeffrey Short was an Oklahoma City Councilman for Asher and former ICE agent who was charged with molesting teenage girls. He faced multiple counts of lewd acts with minors and sexual battery. The charges alleged he preyed on vulnerable teenage victims. Short was arrested in 2019 after an investigation by local authorities.
