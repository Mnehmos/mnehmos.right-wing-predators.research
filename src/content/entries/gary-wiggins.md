## Description

Gary Wiggins and his wife Meghan Wiggins operated conversion therapy homes for LGBTQ youth in Texas. Along with Meghan, he was indicted for human trafficking of boys. The couple ran Restoration Youth Academy and Anchor of Hope, where they allegedly trafficked vulnerable young people. Both faced serious federal charges for exploiting minors.
