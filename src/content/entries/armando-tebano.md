## Description

Schenectady County Republican Chairman and county elections commissioner Armando Tebano pleaded guilty in February 2007 to harassment in connection with allegations he molested a 14-year-old girl at his home. He was sentenced to 250 hours community service and fined $295. The plea was a reduction from original charges of sexual abuse and forcible touching.
