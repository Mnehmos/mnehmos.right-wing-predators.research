## Description

Ciro Adams was Wilmington, Delaware's Republican City Councilman who was accused of sexual and racial harassment in a lawsuit against the city. The allegations described a pattern of inappropriate conduct and discriminatory behavior. Adams faced calls to resign over the harassment allegations. The lawsuit sought damages for creating a hostile work environment.
