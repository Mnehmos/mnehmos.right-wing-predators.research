## Description

Paul Edward Acton Bowen was an Alabama evangelical religious leader who founded Acton Bowen Outreach ministries. He was convicted of child molestation and sentenced to consecutive maximum sentences totaling more than 1,000 years in prison. Prosecutors sought to have him declared a sexually violent predator. Bowen had used his position as a religious leader to gain access to victims.
