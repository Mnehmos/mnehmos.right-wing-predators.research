## Overview

U.S. Representative Robert Bauman (R-MD) was charged on October 3, 1980, with soliciting sex from a 16-year-old male prostitute. Despite being a staunch conservative who often criticized American morality, charges were dropped after he completed court-supervised rehabilitation. He lost his re-election bid in November 1980.
