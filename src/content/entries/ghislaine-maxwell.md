## Overview

Ghislaine Maxwell appears throughout the released archive as Jeffrey Epstein's associate, co-defendant, and alleged recruiter-groomer of minor victims.

## Description

The archive includes Maxwell's federal indictment, which alleges that she worked with Epstein to identify, befriend, groom, and abuse underage girls across several residences. Bail and trial filings in the archive treat Maxwell as a central defendant in the trafficking case, and victim statements filed with the court describe her as an active participant in manipulation and abuse rather than a peripheral social contact. This entry is grounded in the indictment, docket, and victim-facing court material contained in the released files.
