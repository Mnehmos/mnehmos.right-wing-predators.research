## Overview

Former Speaker of the House Dennis Hastert sexually abused high school students while working as a wrestling coach. He was convicted in 2016 of structuring bank withdrawals to evade currency reporting requirements, related to paying hush money to victims of his abuse.

## Description

Former Speaker of the House Dennis Hastert sexually abused high school students while working as a wrestling coach. He was convicted in 2016 of structuring bank withdrawals to evade currency reporting requirements, related to paying hush money to victims of his abuse. He received a 15-month prison sentence.
