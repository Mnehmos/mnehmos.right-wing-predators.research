## Description

Cory Green was a Republican candidate for Utah's 1st congressional district who compared himself to Brett Kavanaugh, claiming he was as innocent as the Supreme Court justice. However, Green had pleaded guilty to sexual battery in the past. His claims of innocence contradicted his guilty plea. The revelation damaged his congressional campaign.
