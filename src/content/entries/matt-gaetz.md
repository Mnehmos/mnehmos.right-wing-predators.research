## Description

Matt Gaetz is a Florida Republican congressman and Trump loyalist who was investigated by the Department of Justice for sex trafficking and having sexual relations with a minor. Fellow Florida Republican Chris Latvala accused Gaetz of creating a game where state legislators got points for sleeping with aides, interns, lobbyists, and married legislators. A witness confirmed Gaetz was told he had sex with a 17-year-old. The investigation examined whether Gaetz violated federal sex trafficking laws.
