## Description

Jodie Laubenberg was a Texas Republican state representative and anti-abortion activist who promoted rape culture by claiming rape kits prevent pregnancy. She argued there was no need for rape exceptions to abortion bans because "rape kits" performed dilation and curettage. Her statement revealed a fundamental misunderstanding of both rape kits and pregnancy. The comments were widely mocked and criticized.
