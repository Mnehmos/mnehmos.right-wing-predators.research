## Description

Daniel Rodimer was a Republican House candidate and former professional wrestler who was involved in a violent incident at a Waffle House. He claimed he was defending women from a bully, but police and witnesses said Rodimer was harassing women and attacked a man who asked him to leave them alone. Despite his violent history, he ran for Congress in Nevada and Texas.
