## Description

Meghan Wiggins and her husband Gary Wiggins ran anti-gay conversion therapy homes in Texas. They were indicted in 2019 for human trafficking of boys at their facilities, Restoration Youth Academy and Anchor of Hope. The couple allegedly exploited vulnerable LGBTQ youth sent to their programs. Federal prosecutors charged them with serious trafficking violations.
