## Description

Mark Huffman was an Ohio Republican state senator who made sexually offensive jokes about women legislators at the same going-away party as Bill Seitz. The crude comments targeted female colleagues and created a hostile environment. Huffman issued an apology after public backlash. The incident exposed ongoing issues with sexism among Ohio Republican legislators.
