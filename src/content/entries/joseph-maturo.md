## Description

Joseph Maturo was Republican Mayor of East Haven, Connecticut who faced multiple sexual harassment allegations. The town spent significant money defending and then settling sexual harassment charges against him. Additional allegations emerged after the initial settlement. Maturo retired in 2019 as more complaints surfaced.
