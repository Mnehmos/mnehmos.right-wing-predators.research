## Overview

Lesley Groff appears in the released archive as an Epstein associate named among the co-conspirators shielded by the plea framework and described in victim filings as facilitating abuse.

## Description

Archive copies of the Epstein non-prosecution and plea materials identify Lesley Groff as one of the potential co-conspirators protected from prosecution. Separate interrogatory material in the archive alleges that Groff helped facilitate meetings between Jane Doe and Epstein and bought gifts on Epstein's behalf, placing her in a hands-on support role inside the abuse network. This entry treats Groff as an archive-backed enabler whose significance comes from facilitation and protection, not from a peripheral social connection.
