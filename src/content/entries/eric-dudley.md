## Description

Eric Dudley was an anti-gay Anglican priest who sexually harassed and abused young adult men through abuse of his power as a religious leader. Dudley had left the Episcopal Church to found an Anglican congregation over opposition to LGBTQ rights. Multiple men came forward with allegations of inappropriate conduct. The hypocrisy of his anti-gay stance while abusing men was noted by critics.
