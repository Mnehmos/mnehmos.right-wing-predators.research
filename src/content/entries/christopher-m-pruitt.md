## Overview

Christopher M. Pruitt, former pastor of the evangelical Our Father's House (Nazarene) church in Portland, Oregon, was indicted on charges of sexually abusing a minor.

## Description

Pruitt served as pastor of Our Father's House, a Nazarene church in Portland, Oregon, before being indicted by a Multnomah County grand jury on multiple counts of sexual abuse. Portland Police confirmed the investigation resulted from allegations by a former member of the congregation.
