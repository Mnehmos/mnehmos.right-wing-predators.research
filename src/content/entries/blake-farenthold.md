## Description

U.S. Representative Blake Farenthold (R-TX) resigned from Congress in April 2018 after it was revealed he used $84,000 in taxpayer money to settle a sexual harassment lawsuit filed by his former communications director. The lawsuit alleged gender discrimination, sexual harassment, and creating a hostile work environment.
