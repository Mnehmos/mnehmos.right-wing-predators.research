## Description

Guy Roberts was a general and former Assistant Secretary of Defense for Nuclear, Chemical, and Biological Defense Programs who was found to have sexually harassed staff members. A Pentagon investigation in 2019 substantiated multiple allegations of inappropriate conduct. Despite his senior position, Roberts faced accountability for creating a hostile work environment. He had held one of the most sensitive positions in the Defense Department.
