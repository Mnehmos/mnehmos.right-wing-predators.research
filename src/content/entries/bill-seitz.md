## Description

Bill Seitz was an Ohio Republican state representative who made sexually offensive jokes about women legislators at a going-away party. The crude remarks were part of a pattern of disrespectful behavior toward female colleagues. Seitz later apologized after the comments became public. The incident highlighted persistent sexism in state legislatures.
