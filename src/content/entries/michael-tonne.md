## Description

Michael Tonne was the associate children's minister at The Village Church and staff at the Children's Bible Camp. While at the camp, he allegedly raped an eleven-year-old girl. The Village Church was more concerned about protecting its reputation than the little girl. In the end, the prosecution declined to move forward with only the child's testimony and dismissed the charges.
