## Description

Former Kentucky judge and Trump Campbell County campaign chair Tim Nolan pleaded guilty in 2018 to 21 counts including human trafficking and child sex trafficking involving 19 victims (7 minors). He used his position and threatened victims with arrest unless they performed sex acts. He was sentenced to 20 years in federal prison.
