## Description

Tim Braun was Republican Sandusky County Prosecutor in Ohio who pleaded guilty in 2019 to a reduced charge in a plea deal over a sexual assault complaint. Despite the conviction, he was allowed to keep his job as prosecutor. The lenient treatment sparked outrage about different standards for those in power. Braun had been accused of sexually assaulting a woman at a bar.
