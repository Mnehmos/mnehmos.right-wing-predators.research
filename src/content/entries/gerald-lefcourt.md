## Overview

Gerald Lefcourt appears in the released archive as one of Jeffrey Epstein's New York criminal defense attorneys and a signatory to the non-prosecution framework that protected Epstein and his network.

## Description

Archive copies of Epstein's non-prosecution agreement and addendum repeatedly identify Gerald Lefcourt as counsel to Jeffrey Epstein. The released files include signature pages and later court-filed reproductions of the same agreement, showing Lefcourt inside the legal team that secured Epstein's unusually lenient federal resolution in 2007. This entry treats Lefcourt as an archive-backed shield figure in the plea structure that limited accountability for Epstein and potential co-conspirators.
