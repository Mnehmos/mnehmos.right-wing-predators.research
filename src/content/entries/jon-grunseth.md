## Description

Jon Grunseth is a Republican businessman from Minnesota who withdrew his candidacy for Minnesota governor in 2017 after serious allegations surfaced. Multiple women accused Grunseth of going swimming in the nude with four underage girls, including his own daughter. The scandal ended his political ambitions after details of the alleged inappropriate behavior with minors became public.
