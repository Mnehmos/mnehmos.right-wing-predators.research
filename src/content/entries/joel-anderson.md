## Description

Joel Anderson was a California Republican state senator who was reprimanded for sexually harassing and threatening a woman lobbyist. The Senate Rules Committee found his conduct violated workplace harassment policies. Anderson engaged in unwanted sexual comments and intimidation. He continued to serve despite the formal reprimand.
