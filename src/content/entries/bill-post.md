## Description

Bill Post was an Oregon Republican state representative accused of sexual harassment, including touching a woman under a table and mocking sexual assault victims. He made dismissive comments about assault allegations on social media. Multiple women complained about inappropriate behavior. Post faced ethics complaints but remained in the legislature for a time.
