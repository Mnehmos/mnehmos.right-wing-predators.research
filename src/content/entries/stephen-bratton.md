## Description

Stephen Bratton was a Texas Baptist minister who advocated charging women who get abortions with murder. He was arrested in 2019 and charged with continuous sexual abuse of a child under 14 years old. The allegations involved multiple incidents of sexual abuse. Bratton had served as pastor at Grace Baptist Church in Texas.
