## Description

Travis Kalanick was Uber CEO and Ayn Rand devotee who briefly served on Trump's economic advisory council. He was forced to resign as CEO in 2017 over widespread sexual harassment allegations at Uber. The company faced numerous lawsuits alleging a toxic workplace culture that enabled harassment. Kalanick's leadership was criticized for tolerating misconduct.
