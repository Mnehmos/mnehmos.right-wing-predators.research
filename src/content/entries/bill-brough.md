## Description

Bill Brough was a California Republican assemblyman who faced multiple complaints of sexual harassment from women, including fellow Republicans. The Orange County GOP asked him to withdraw from his 2020 reelection bid due to the allegations. He also faced ethics charges related to misuse of campaign funds. Multiple women described a pattern of inappropriate behavior spanning years.
