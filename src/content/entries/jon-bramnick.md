## Description

Jon Bramnick is a New Jersey Republican state senator who made a legal career enabling sexual predators to clean up their records and prey again. His law firm's website bragged about his success discrediting victims of sexual assault until public outcry forced him to remove the content. Governor Murphy called his strategy "disgusting." Bramnick continued to serve in the legislature despite the controversy.
