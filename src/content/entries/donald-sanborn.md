## Description

Donald Sanborn is an extreme rightwing traditionalist Catholic bishop who blamed women for being raped because they don't wear skirts to their knees and three-quarter length sleeves. He argued that immodest dress causes men to rape women. Sanborn runs a traditionalist seminary and has promoted extreme views on women's behavior. His victim-blaming statements sparked outrage.
