## Description

Dennis Nielsen was a Johnston County gun shop owner and Republican candidate for North Carolina Senate District 11 who faced domestic violence allegations from his wife. The accusations surfaced during his political campaign. Court records documented the allegations. Nielsen's candidacy was damaged by the domestic violence claims.
