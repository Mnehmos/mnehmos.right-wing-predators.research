## Description

Randall Hunt was Republican Governor Ron DeSantis' appointee to the Florida Lottery who resigned in 2020 after a background check revealed a domestic violence complaint. The vetting failure embarrassed the DeSantis administration. Hunt stepped down before taking office. The incident raised questions about the administration's screening process.
