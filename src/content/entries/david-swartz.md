## Description

GOP Ohio County (Richland County, Ohio) Commissioner David Swartz, age 66, pleaded guilty to two felony counts of sexual battery. The abuse of a girl began when she was 6 years old or younger, with the guilty plea covering a four-year period between January 2000 and January 2004. Swartz was sentenced to eight years in prison and must register as a sex offender.
