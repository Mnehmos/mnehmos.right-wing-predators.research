## Description

S. Vance Wilkins was a Virginia Republican Delegate and former House Speaker who retired nearly 20 years ago after sexual harassment allegations and paying off an accuser. Despite the scandal, he remained a Republican party player. In 2014, reports surfaced that he was considering a political comeback. His continued involvement in GOP politics demonstrated the party's willingness to overlook sexual misconduct.
