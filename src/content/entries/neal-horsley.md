## Description

Neal Horsley (1944-2015) was a militant anti-abortion activist and Christian Reconstructionist. In 2005, Horsley admitted during an interview that he engaged in bestiality as an adolescent, stating "When you grow up on a farm in Georgia, your first girlfriend is a mule." He also boasted about having sex with men and abusing drugs. Horsley served a 30-month federal prison sentence in 1973 for possession of three gallons of hashish oil. He created and maintained the "Nuremberg Files" website, which listed the names, addresses, and other details of abortion providers, health care workers, politicians, and judges who supported abortion rights. He also included a list of providers who had been wounded or killed, effectively creating a target list for violence.
