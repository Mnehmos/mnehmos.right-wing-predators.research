## Description

Kevin Etherington, Assistant District Attorney in Payne and Logan counties, Oklahoma, was arrested in November 2022 and charged with child pornography possession. The Oklahoma State Bureau of Investigation's ICAC unit received cybertips of suspected child sexual abuse material on his Google Drive account and discovered at least 153 videos, screenshots, and photos of prepubescent girls being sexually abused. Etherington faced one count of aggravated possession of child pornography and one count of peeping tom felony after evidence emerged of voyeuristic photographs of children.
