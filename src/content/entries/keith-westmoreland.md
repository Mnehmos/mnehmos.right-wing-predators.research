## Overview

Tennessee state representative and former Sullivan County executive Keith Westmoreland was arrested in 2002 on seven felony counts of lewd and lascivious exhibition to girls under age 16. He exposed his genitals to children at a hotel pool in Sandestin, Florida. He later committed suicide.
