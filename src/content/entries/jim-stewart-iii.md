## Description

Jim Stewart III was a Kentucky Republican state representative accused of sexually harassing a legislative staffer in 2015. A memo detailed allegations that Stewart made unwanted sexual advances and created a hostile work environment. The case was settled quietly, but details later became public. Stewart faced criticism for his conduct but remained in office.
