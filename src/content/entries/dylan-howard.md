## Description

Dylan Howard was editor of the National Enquirer and other American Media Inc. publications who faced sexual harassment allegations from multiple women. He was featured in Ronan Farrow's book "Catch and Kill" for his role in covering up sexual predator allegations on behalf of powerful men. Howard allegedly used his media position to suppress stories and intimidate victims.
