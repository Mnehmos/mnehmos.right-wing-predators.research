## Overview

Haley Robson appears in the released archive as a recruiter of minors for Jeffrey Epstein and a named defendant in related complaints.

## Description

The Palm Beach affidavits in the archive say Haley Robson recruited girls, including minors, to come to Epstein's residence for so-called massages that turned into sexual abuse. Police reports and search-warrant material in the archive list Robson among the suspects or associates tied to the case, and a sworn interview describes her own account of recruiting girls into Epstein's orbit. Later civil filings in the released files name Robson as a defendant in conspiracy and emotional-distress claims arising from the same conduct.
