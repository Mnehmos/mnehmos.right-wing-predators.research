## Overview

Oregon Christian Coalition chairman Lou Beres admitted in 2006 to sexually touching multiple girls when they were minors. He confessed to police that he molested a 13-year-old girl and touched a 16-17-year-old friend of his daughter in 1976-77. Three women came forward in 2005. No charges were filed due to statute of limitations, but he was sued in civil court.
