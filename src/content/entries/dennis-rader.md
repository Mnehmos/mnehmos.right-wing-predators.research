## Overview

Dennis Rader, the BTK serial killer, was arrested on February 25, 2005. He was a Park City compliance supervisor, Boy Scout leader, and was elected president of Christ Lutheran Church council in January 2005. He murdered 10 people between 1974 and 1991.
