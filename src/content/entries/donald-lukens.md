## Overview

U.S. Representative Donald "Buz" Lukens (R-OH) was convicted in 1989 of contributing to the delinquency of a minor after paying a 16-year-old girl $40 for sex. He was convicted again in 1996 on bribery and conspiracy charges related to his time in Congress and sentenced to 30 months in prison.
