## Description

Steven Pankey was a former Republican candidate for Idaho governor who was convicted in 2022 for the 1984 kidnapping and murder of 12-year-old Jonelle Matthews. Her body was found in 2019, 35 years after she disappeared. Pankey had been a suspect at the time due to prior rape allegations. He was found guilty of kidnapping and felony murder after a lengthy investigation.
