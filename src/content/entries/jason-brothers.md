## Description

Jason Brothers was an evangelist and pastor in Georgia who raped a 14-year-old girl he was counseling. He received only probation despite the serious charges, benefiting from Georgia House Speaker David Ralston's legal maneuvering to delay his trial. The case sparked outrage over preferential treatment and abuse of the judicial system. Brothers avoided prison time thanks to political intervention.
