## Description

Robert Ray was the Whitewater prosecutor who investigated the Clintons and ran as a Republican candidate for U.S. Senate in New Jersey in 2002. He was charged with stalking after allegedly following and harassing a woman. Ray withdrew from the Senate race following the charges. The incident damaged his reputation as a prosecutor.
