## Description

Steve King was an Iowa Republican congressman who infamously claimed that humanity would not exist without rape and incest while defending his opposition to abortion exceptions. He questioned when rape and incest became a problem, suggesting they were necessary for population growth. The comments sparked outrage and led Republicans to strip him of committee assignments. King had a long history of racist and extremist statements.
