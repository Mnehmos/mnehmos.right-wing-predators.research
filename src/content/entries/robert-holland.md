## Description

Robert Holland, 76, was a longtime Republican Township Supervisor in Bedminster Township, Pennsylvania and head of groundskeeper services at Pine Run Community Assisted Living Facility, where he supervised five employees. Holland was charged with multiple felony and misdemeanor counts of sexual assault after admitting to sexually assaulting a 92-year-old nursing home resident who was unable to give meaningful consent. Holland moved the victim from her alarmed wheelchair to a nearby sofa where he fondled and engaged in other sexual acts. Nursing assistant Martha Palena witnessed the assault and reported it. Holland's assaults spanned approximately six months from November 2005 until his discovery. He was arraigned on charges including aggravated indecent assault (2nd degree felony), institutional sexual assault (3rd degree felony), and three counts of misdemeanor indecent assault.
