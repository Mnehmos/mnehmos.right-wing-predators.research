## Overview

Oklahoma state senator and Donald Trump's 2016 Oklahoma campaign chair Ralph Shortey was arrested in March 2017 after being found in a motel room with a 17-year-old boy. He pleaded guilty in 2017 to child sex trafficking and was sentenced to 15 years in federal prison in 2018.
