## Description

Paxton Singer was a youth pastor at anti-gay Harvest Bible Chapel who was accused of sexual misconduct with young people in the church. The allegations led to his termination from the church. Singer had been involved in youth ministry despite the church's condemnation of LGBTQ individuals. The case highlighted hypocrisy within conservative evangelical institutions.
