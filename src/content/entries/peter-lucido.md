## Description

Peter Lucido was a Michigan Republican state senator who made sexual innuendo in front of a group of high school boys, suggesting they might "have a lot of fun" with a young female reporter. His comment was widely interpreted as a rape threat. Later, additional women accused Lucido of sexual harassment and unwanted touching. Investigations found credible allegations of misconduct.
