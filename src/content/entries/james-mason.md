## Description

White nationalist and influential neo-Nazi figure James Mason was arrested in the late 1980s-1990s and found to have nude images of a 15-year-old girl. In 1994, he was charged with sexual exploitation of a minor. He was convicted on weapons charges. Mason is best known as author of 'Siege', influential neo-Nazi manifesto advocating for white supremacist revolution.
