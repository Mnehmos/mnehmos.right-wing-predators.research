## Description

Denton County, Texas, Republican Constable Larry Dale Floyd was arrested in July 2005 for soliciting sex with an 8-year-old girl. He drove to Fremont County, Colorado, to meet a woman he thought would offer him her daughter for sex. He faced charges of soliciting child prostitution, enticement of a child, and attempted sexual assault. He was sentenced in 2016.
