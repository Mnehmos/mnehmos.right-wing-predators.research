## Description

Robert Murray is CEO of Murray Energy and major Republican donor who was accused of sexual harassment. He is famously litigious and has sued journalists and critics, including John Oliver. The harassment allegations received limited coverage, which some attributed to fear of his lawsuits. Murray sued women's lawyers for sharing information to advance their harassment case.
