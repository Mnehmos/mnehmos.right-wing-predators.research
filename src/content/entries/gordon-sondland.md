## Description

Gordon Sondland was a Republican mega-donor and Trump's Ambassador to the European Union who was accused of sexual misconduct and retaliation by three women. The allegations emerged during his testimony in Trump's first impeachment inquiry. Multiple women described a pattern of unwanted advances and professional retaliation when they rejected him. Sondland denied the allegations.
