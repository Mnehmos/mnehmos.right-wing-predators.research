## Description

David Ralston was Georgia Republican Speaker of the House who abused his power to help criminal defendants evade justice. An investigation revealed he used a legal ploy called "Ralston's Rule" to delay trials indefinitely, including for rapist Jason Brothers. He leveraged his legislative position to manipulate the court system for over 20 clients. The scandal exposed corruption at the highest levels of Georgia Republican leadership.
