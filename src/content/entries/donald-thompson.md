## Description

Donald Thompson was a Creek County District Judge in Oklahoma who was convicted in June 2006 of four counts of indecent exposure. Between 2001 and 2003, his court reporter testified that she witnessed Thompson expose himself at least 15 times while on the bench. The allegations became public when a police officer in Thompson's courtroom heard suspicious pumping sounds and discovered that Thompson was using a 'penis pump' during proceedings. Thompson was sentenced to four years in prison and served 20 months before his release. He was required to register as a sex offender and was disbarred by the Oklahoma Supreme Court, which also stripped him of his pension. Thompson claimed the device was a joke from a hunting buddy and that he may have absentmindedly squeezed it, but never intentionally used it to masturbate.
