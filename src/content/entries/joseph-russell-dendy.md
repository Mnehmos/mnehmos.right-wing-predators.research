## Description

Cobb County GOP Chairman Joseph Russell Dendy pleaded guilty in 2017 to 13 charges including child molestation, enticing a child for indecent purposes, and aggravated sexual battery. He received a life sentence with 30 years to serve. The crimes occurred from 2004 to 2011. He was arrested in May 2016.
