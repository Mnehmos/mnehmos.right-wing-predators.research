## Description

Andrew Knarr was a GOP legislative aide to Colorado state Senator Jim Smallwood who was fired for joking about sexual assault. Knarr made inappropriate comments that trivialized sexual violence. The remarks came during heightened attention to the #MeToo movement. Senator Smallwood terminated Knarr's employment after learning of the misconduct.
