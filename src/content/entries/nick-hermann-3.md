## Description

Nick Hermann was Republican Vanderburgh County Prosecutor in Indiana who was accused of sexual harassment and retaliation against an employee who reported him. A former employee alleged Hermann made unwanted advances and then retaliated when she rejected him. The allegations led to a federal lawsuit and damaged his reputation. Hermann denied the allegations.
