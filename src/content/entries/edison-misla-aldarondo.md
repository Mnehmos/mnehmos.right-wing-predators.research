Puerto Rico House Speaker Edison Misla Aldarondo was charged in 2002 with rape of a 17-year-old friend of his stepdaughter. He was convicted of attempted rape, sexual abuse of a minor, and four counts of lewd conduct for molesting his stepdaughter beginning when she was 9 years old.
